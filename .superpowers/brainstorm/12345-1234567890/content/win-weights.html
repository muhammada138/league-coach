The "Anchor" Effect

Even in "balanced" lobbies, a Gold 1 player vs. a Gold 4 player is a ~10% skill gap. Rank acts as the baseline. If ranks are identical, this 30% "cancels out" (diff = 0), letting other stats decide.

The "Momentum" Signal

Form (25%) and Recent WR (20%) catch the "smurf" or "tilter." A Bronze 1 player with 80% Recent WR is actually a Silver/Gold player in transition. This 45% total momentum is what actually shifts the needle.
