Why it works

  • Rank is Stable: 30% of the model is rank. In a lobby, we can guess this with high confidence (±1 division).
  • Team Means: Players usually play at the level of their team, so the mean is a decent proxy.

What we lose

  • "The Carry" Signal: If the hidden player is on a 10-game win streak with a 70% champ WR, we miss the "Red Alert" signal.
  • First-Timers: We can't tell if the hidden player is first-timing a champion (usually a ~40% WR).
